# The Crimson Tart

## Ingredients
### Base
- 20ml fresh lemon juice
- Splash of pomegranate juice
- Recess Raspberry Lemon water
- 2 dashes Orange bitters or 1 dash Angostura
- Garnish: Rosemary sprig and frozen raspberries
- Optional: Lemon zest and salt (for the rim)

### Alcohol
- All Base ingredients
- 60ml Bourbon or Gin

## Directions
### Base
1. (Optional) Rim the glass with a mix of lemon zest and salt.
2. Fill a glass with plenty of ice.
3. Add the lemon juice and pomegranate juice.
4. Top with the Raspberry Lemon water and stir gently.
5. Add the bitters.
6. Slap the rosemary sprig between your hands to release the oils and add to the glass with the frozen raspberries.

### Alcohol
1. Follow the steps above, adding the Bourbon or Gin to the glass before topping with the Raspberry Lemon water.
