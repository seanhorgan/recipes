# The Golden Hour

*A crisp, effervescent drink that bridges the gap between citrus and spice.*

## Ingredients
### Base
- 20ml fresh lime juice
- 10ml honey syrup (equal parts honey and warm water)
- Recess Ginger Lime (or a crisp Ginger Beer)
- 2 dashes Grapefruit bitters
- A pinch of sea salt
- Garnish: Fresh ginger slice, lime wheel, and a slapped sage leaf

### Alcohol
- 60ml Tequila Blanco or Mezcal

## Directions
### Base
1. Add the lime juice, honey syrup, and a tiny pinch of sea salt to a glass. Stir until integrated.
2. Fill the glass with large ice cubes.
3. Top with the Ginger Lime water and stir gently.
4. Add the bitters on top.
5. Slap a fresh sage leaf to release the oils and tuck it into the ice along with the ginger and lime.

### Alcohol
1. Follow the steps above, adding the Tequila or Mezcal to the glass before adding the ice and Ginger Lime water.
