# The Rose Garden

## Ingredients
### Base
- 3–4 fresh basil leaves
- 15ml fresh lime juice
- Recess Strawberry Rose water
- 2–3 dashes Rhubarb or Peychaud’s bitters
- Garnish: Fresh strawberry slice and a sprig of basil
- Optional: Sugar and dried rose petals (for the rim)

### Alcohol
- 60ml Gin or Vodka

## Directions
### Base
1. (Optional) Rim the glass with a mix of sugar and dried rose petals.
2. In a tall glass, lightly muddle the basil leaves with the lime juice.
3. Fill the glass with ice and pour the Strawberry Rose water over it.
4. Add the bitters and stir gently.
5. Garnish with a strawberry slice and a sprig of basil.

### Alcohol
1. Follow the steps above, adding the Gin or Vodka to the glass after muddling the basil and before adding the ice and Strawberry Rose water.
