# Virgin Paloma

## Ingredients
### Base
- 1/4 cup fresh grapefruit juice
- Recess Grapefruit Paloma (to top)
- Juice of half a lime
- Garnish: Grapefruit wedge and Kosher salt (for the rim)

### Alcohol
- 60ml Blanco Tequila

## Directions
### Base
1. Run a grapefruit wedge along half the rim of a highball glass and dip it into Kosher salt.
2. Fill the glass with ice cubes.
3. Add the fresh grapefruit juice and lime juice.
4. Top with Recess Grapefruit Paloma and stir gently.
5. Garnish with a grapefruit wedge.

### Alcohol
1. Follow the steps above, adding the Tequila to the glass before topping with the Recess Grapefruit Paloma.
