# Virgin Paloma

## Ingredients
### Base
- 1/4 cup fresh grapefruit juice
- 1/4 cup all-natural cactus juice
- Recess Grapefruit Rose water (to top)
- Juice of half a lime
- 1 tsp superfine sugar (optional if using Recess)
- Garnish: Grapefruit wedge and Kosher salt (for the rim)

### Alcohol
- 60ml Blanco Tequila

## Directions
### Base
1. Run a grapefruit wedge along half the rim of a highball glass and dip it into Kosher salt.
2. In the glass, combine the grapefruit juice, lime juice, and superfine sugar. Stir until the sugar is dissolved.
3. Fill the glass with ice cubes and pour in the cactus juice.
4. Top with Recess Grapefruit Rose water and stir gently.
5. Garnish with a grapefruit wedge.

### Alcohol
1. Follow the steps above, adding the Tequila to the glass before topping with the Recess Grapefruit Rose water.
