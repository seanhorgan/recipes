# Planning Protocols

## Family Profile
- **Total:** 4 People (Ali, Sean, Rose, Will).
- **Kids:** Rose & Will (11 years old).
- **Dietary (Sean):** Pescatarian (No red meat, pork, or chicken). Family defaults to Pescatarian for shared meals.
- **Dietary (Ali):** Gluten-Free (GF). Not celiac, but prefers GF. Default to everyone eating GF for the main shared dish to keep it simple.
- **Preferences (Will):** Dislikes mushrooms (can be an ingredient, but don't center the dish around them).

## Scheduling & Prep
- **Sunday Prep focus:** Optimize for meals that can be partially or fully prepped on Sunday.
- **Fast Weeknight Execution:** Aim for 10-15 minute active assembly/cook time on weeknights to accommodate sports and tutoring schedules.
- **Unified Meals:** Always plan exactly ONE meal for the entire family. No split menus.

## Variety & Scaling
- **Family Sizing:** Default to 5 large sweet potatoes (1 per person + 1 extra for growth).
- **Protein Boost:** Proactively increase protein in vegetarian/lighter meals (e.g., parmesan, extra ricotta, mashed chickpeas) for growing kids.
- **Kid Preferences:** Substitute Tahini with Lemon-Garlic Yogurt sauce.
- **Preparation:** For mashes, use unpeeled yellow potatoes for texture and nutrients.
