# Chicken Sausage & White Bean Cassoulet

## Ingredients
12 oz chicken sausage (sliced), 2 cans white beans, 1 can crushed tomatoes, leeks, cooked quinoa.

## Directions
Sauté sausage with prepped leeks. Add beans and tomatoes. Simmer for 10 mins. Serve in deep bowls over warm quinoa.
