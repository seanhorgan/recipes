# Creamy Butternut Squash & White Bean Soup

## Ingredients
1 medium butternut squash (cubed/roasted), 1 can cannellini beans (rinsed), 4 cups GF veg broth, 1 tsp dried thyme, GF baguette slices.

## Directions
Simmer roasted squash, beans, and broth for 10 mins. Add thyme. Use an immersion blender until smooth. Serve with olive-oil-toasted GF crustinis.
