# Crispy Turmeric Salmon & Sweet Potato "Steaks"

*High-protein, anti-inflammatory meal with complex carbs.*

## Ingredients
- 1.5 lbs Salmon fillets
- 5 large Sweet potatoes (cubed; 1 per person + 1 extra for growth stage)
- 1 bag Spinach
- 1 tsp Turmeric
- Olive oil
- Salt and pepper

## Directions
1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
2. Peel and cube the 5 large sweet potatoes; toss with oil/salt and roast for 25 mins.
3. Rub salmon with turmeric and salt.
4. Pan-sear salmon skin-side down for 4 mins. Add pre-roasted sweet potatoes to the pan to crisp up.
5. Flip salmon and cook 2 mins more.
6. Stir in spinach at the end until wilted.
