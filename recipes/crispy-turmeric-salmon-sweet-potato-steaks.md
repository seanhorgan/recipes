# Crispy Turmeric Salmon & Sweet Potato "Steaks"

## Ingredients
1.5 lbs salmon, roasted cubed sweet potatoes, 1 bag spinach, turmeric, olive oil.

## Directions
(Sunday Prep) Peel and cube 2 sweet potatoes; roast at 400°F for 25 mins. (Night of) Rub salmon with turmeric and salt. Pan-sear salmon skin-side down for 4 mins. Add pre-roasted sweet potatoes to the pan to crisp up. Flip salmon and cook 2 mins more. Stir in spinach at the end to wilt.
