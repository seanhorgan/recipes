# Greek-Style Stuffed Bell Peppers

## Ingredients
4 bell peppers (halved), 1 lb ground turkey, 1 cup cooked quinoa, 1 cup tomato sauce, 1 tsp oregano.

## Directions
Brown turkey with oregano. Mix with quinoa and sauce. Stuff peppers and bake at 375°F for 25 mins.
