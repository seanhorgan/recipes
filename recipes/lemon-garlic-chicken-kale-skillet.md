# Lemon-Garlic White Fish & Kale Skillet

*A quick, high-iron pescatarian skillet with bright citrus notes.*

## Ingredients
- 1.5 lbs White Fish (Cod, Tilapia, or Halibut), cut into bite-sized chunks
- 1 bunch Kale (chopped)
- 2 tbsp Olive oil
- Lemon-Garlic Yogurt sauce (from protocols) or Zing Dressing (Jar A)
- 1 can Cannellini beans (added for kid growth/protein)

## Directions
1. Sauté fish chunks in olive oil until golden and just cooked through (about 5-6 mins). Remove fish from pan.
2. Add kale, rinsed cannellini beans, and 2 tbsp water to the pan; cover for 2 mins to steam the kale.
3. Return fish to the pan and pour in the dressing or yogurt sauce.
4. Simmer for 1 min until everything is coated and warm.
