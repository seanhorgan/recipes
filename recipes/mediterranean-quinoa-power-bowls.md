# Mediterranean Quinoa Power Bowls

## Ingredients
2 cups cooked quinoa, 1 can chickpeas, 1 cucumber (diced), 1 cup cherry tomatoes, 1/4 cup Kalamata olives, 2 oz feta, Simple Vinaigrette.

## Directions
Toss quinoa and chickpeas with fresh vegetables and olives. Dress with olive oil, lemon, and honey.
