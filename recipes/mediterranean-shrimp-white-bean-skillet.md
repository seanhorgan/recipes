# Mediterranean Shrimp & White Bean Skillet Bake

*A one-pan braise using heart-healthy fats and quick protein.*

## Ingredients
- 1 lb Shrimp (peeled/deveined)
- 2 cans White beans (rinsed)
- 1 jar Roasted red peppers (sliced)
- 1 bag Spinach or Kale
- 2 cloves Garlic (minced)
- 1/2 cup Feta cheese
- Juice of 1/2 Lemon

## Directions
1. Sauté garlic in a large skillet with olive oil.
2. Add the 2 cans of white beans and roasted red peppers. Simmer for 5 mins, lightly smashing some beans to thicken the base.
3. Stir in the greens until wilted.
4. Nestle shrimp into the mixture. Cover and cook for 4-5 mins until shrimp are pink.
5. Top with crumbled feta and a squeeze of lemon.
