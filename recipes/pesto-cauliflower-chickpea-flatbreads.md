# Pesto-Roasted Cauliflower & Chickpea Flatbreads

*A twist on the Zucchini Flatbreads using the twins' favorite roasted veggies and pesto.*

## Ingredients
- 2-4 GF Flatbreads
- 1 head Cauliflower (cut into small florets)
- 1 can Chickpeas (rinsed)
- 1/2 cup GF Pesto
- 1/2 cup Ricotta or Goat cheese
- Handful of Arugula

## Directions
1. Toss cauliflower and chickpeas with olive oil and salt. Roast at 400°F for 20 mins until crispy.
2. Spread a thin layer of ricotta or goat cheese on the flatbreads.
3. Top with the roasted cauliflower and chickpeas.
4. Drizzle the GF pesto generously over the top.
5. Bake at 425°F for 5-8 mins until flatbread is crisp.
6. Finish with fresh arugula.
