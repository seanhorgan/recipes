# Pesto-Roasted Cauliflower & Chickpea Grain Bowls

*A high-fiber, assembly-style bowl using the twins' favorite roasted veggies and pesto.*

## Ingredients
- 2 cups Quinoa or Sorghum (cooked)
- 1 head Cauliflower (cut into small florets)
- 1 can Chickpeas (rinsed)
- 1/2 cup GF Pesto
- Handful of Arugula
- Optional: Crumbled goat cheese or feta

## Directions
1. Toss cauliflower and chickpeas with olive oil and salt. Roast at 400°F for 20 mins until crispy.
2. Warm the pre-cooked grains (quinoa or sorghum).
3. In a large bowl, layer the grains and top with a big handful of arugula.
4. Add the roasted cauliflower and chickpeas.
5. Drizzle the GF pesto generously over the top and toss lightly.
6. Optional: Top with a sprinkle of cheese.
