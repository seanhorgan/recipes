# Red Lentil Soup & Polenta Fries

## Ingredients
1 quart red lentil soup, 1 bag spinach, 1 tube pre-cooked polenta, 1 apple (sliced), 1/4 cup walnuts, 2 oz feta.

## Directions
Slice polenta into fries; toss with oil and bake at 400°F for 20 mins. Reheat soup and stir in spinach to wilt. Serve with apple/walnut/feta salad.
