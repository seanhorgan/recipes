# Stuffed Sweet Potatoes with Mediterranean Chickpeas

## Ingredients
4–6 large sweet potatoes, 2 cans chickpeas (rinsed), 1/2 cup feta, arugula, Honey-Mustard Citrus Glaze.

## Directions
(Sunday Prep) Roast 4–6 whole potatoes at 400°F for 45 mins until tender. (Night of) Reheat potatoes and slice into thick rounds or split open. Toss chickpeas with 2 tbsp of glaze. Serve "platter style": arrange a bed of arugula, top with sweet potato rounds and chickpeas, and sprinkle with feta. Drizzle generously with Honey-Mustard Citrus Glaze to keep textures distinct.
