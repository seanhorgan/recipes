# Turmeric-Ginger Cod with Roasted Root Mash

*Inspired by the Turmeric Salmon; a warm, anti-inflammatory white fish dish.*

## Ingredients
- 1.5 lbs Cod fillets
- 1 lb Carrots (peeled and chopped)
- 1 lb Yellow potatoes (**skins on**, chopped)
- 1 tsp Turmeric
- 1 tsp Ginger powder (or fresh grated)
- 2 tbsp Olive oil
- Splash of Coconut milk (optional, for the mash)
- Salt and pepper

## Directions
1. Boil the carrots and unpeeled potatoes together until tender (about 15 mins).
2. Drain and mash with olive oil and a splash of coconut milk or broth, **keeping the potato skins in the mash** for texture and nutrients.
3. Rub cod fillets with turmeric, ginger, salt, and pepper.
4. Pan-sear cod in olive oil over medium-high heat for 3-4 mins per side until flaky.
5. Serve the cod over a generous bed of the root mash.
