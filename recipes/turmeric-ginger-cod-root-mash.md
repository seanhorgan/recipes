# Turmeric-Ginger Cod with Roasted Root Mash

*Inspired by the Turmeric Salmon; a warm, anti-inflammatory white fish dish.*

## Ingredients
- 1.5 lbs Cod fillets
- 1 lb Carrots (peeled and chopped)
- 1 lb Yellow potatoes (peeled and chopped)
- 1 tsp Turmeric
- 1 tsp Ginger powder (or fresh grated)
- 2 tbsp Olive oil
- Splash of Coconut milk (optional, for the mash)
- Salt and pepper

## Directions
1. Boil the carrots and potatoes together until tender (about 15 mins). Drain and mash with olive oil and a splash of coconut milk or broth.
2. Rub cod fillets with turmeric, ginger, salt, and pepper.
3. Pan-sear cod in olive oil over medium-high heat for 3-4 mins per side until flaky.
4. Serve the cod over a generous bed of the root mash.
