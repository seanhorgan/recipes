# Warm Quinoa & Beet Bowls

## Ingredients
1.5 cups dry quinoa (cooked), 2 cans beets (rinsed/sliced), 4 oz goat cheese, 1/2 cup walnuts, 2 cups arugula, Zing Dressing (Jar A).

## Directions
Warm cooked quinoa and beets in a pan. Place over a bed of arugula. Top with crumbled goat cheese, walnuts, and a heavy drizzle of Jar A.
