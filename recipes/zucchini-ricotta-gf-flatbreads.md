# Zucchini & Ricotta GF Flatbreads

*A light, pizza-inspired meal with natural fibers.*

## Ingredients
- 2-4 GF Flatbreads
- 1.5 cups Ricotta cheese (increased for protein)
- 1/4 cup Grated Parmesan (added for protein boost)
- 1-2 Zucchini or Summer Squash (sliced into rounds)
- 2 cans Chickpeas (mash into the ricotta for extra protein)
- Olive oil (for drizzling)
- Red pepper flakes
- Handful of Arugula

## Directions
1. In a small bowl, mix the ricotta with parmesan and **2 cans of mashed chickpeas** for a high-protein base.
2. **Brush or drizzle each GF flatbread with olive oil** before adding the spread.
3. Spread the protein-rich cheese mixture evenly over the oiled flatbreads.
4. Layer zucchini or squash rounds on top.
5. **Generously drizzle olive oil over the entire flatbread** and the veggie rounds.
6. Bake at 425°F for 12 mins.
7. Top with fresh arugula.
